CONSTITUTION OF: ArawaK Community Trust on 1st November 2018

Name The name of the Group shall be: ArawaK Community Trust

Aims

Organise and manage family sporting/social/educational events throughout the year that brings about inter- generational participation
Promote the communicational medium of radio through the development of ArawaK Radio
Facilitate the opportunity for BME companies/vendors/charities to market their services and sell their products at such events and via our radio/social media
Facilitate and encourage community cohesion through participation in the events by all members of the local and wider community
Raise funds, for nominated local charities, through specific activities i.e. raffle draws, prize competitions
Encourage the growth and development of young people through volunteering opportunities offered by delivering those events
